    About

    GRS Fantasy Park is a popular amusement park in Mysuru city, offering a wide range of thrill seeking rides, family rides, games and entertainment options.GRS is the only amusement cum water park in and around Mysore. Situated just 15 minutes from Mysore, on the Mysore-Bangalore highway, GRS is easily accessible by road. With 40 acres of plush greenery to call its own, GRS sports a wide range of dry and wet rides that cater to the entire family for a fun filled safe vacation for all. Come visit us we have all the ingredients for that perfect holiday!
    For More Click Here!!

    What to expect at GRS Fantasy Park?

    1. Thrill Rides:Aqua racer, Aqua tornado, Pendulum Slide, Crazy Cruise, Amazonia, Colombia, Music Bob and Dragon’s Den are the intense, adrenaline pumping rides at the Park.
    2. Family Rides: Snow Sledge, Swing Chair, Dashing Car, Float Slides, Red Indian falls, Wave Pool, Aqua Dance Floor and Hawaiian Paradise are the relatively slow paced, fun filled rides for all family members.
    3. Kids Rides: Aqua trail, baby trains, play area, kids pool etc.
    4. Facilities: Changing rooms, restaurants, locker rooms, gift shop, conference rooms etc.

    Ticket

    Adults (above 4'6" in height) INR 899.00
    Children (3' to 4'6" in height)INR 799.00
    Infants (below 3') Free
    Senior Citizens (above 65 years)INR 799.00

    ENTRY AFTER 3.30 PM**

    Adults (above 4'6" in height)INR 749.00
    Children (3' to 4'6" in height)INR 649.00

    Timings to visit GRS Fantasy Park

    • GRS Fantasy Park is open from 10.30 AM till 6 PM on Monday to Saturday.
    • 10.30 AM till 7.30 PM on Sundays and on national holidays.
    It is recommended to spend a full day at Fantasy park.
